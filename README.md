# Business Automation Flow ⚙️

A complete lead management automation that connects your web form, CRM, Google Sheets and WhatsApp into a single hands-free pipeline.

## ✨ What it automates

1. New lead fills out form on your website
2. Lead data saved automatically to Google Sheets
3. WhatsApp notification sent to the sales team instantly
4. Automated welcome message sent to the lead
5. Follow-up reminder scheduled for 24 hours later

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **n8n** — workflow orchestration
- **Google Sheets** — lead database
- **Evolution API** — WhatsApp messaging
- **Webhooks** — real-time triggers

## 📊 Flow Diagram

```
[Web Form] → [n8n Webhook]
                  ↓
         [Save to Google Sheets]
                  ↓
     [Send WhatsApp to sales team]
                  ↓
     [Send welcome message to lead]
                  ↓
     [Schedule 24h follow-up reminder]
```

## ⏱️ Time saved

This automation replaces ~2 hours of manual work per day for a business receiving 10+ leads daily.
